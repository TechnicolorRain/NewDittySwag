Trademark and Intellectual Property Guidelines:

1. Trademark Search: Conduct a comprehensive search to ensure that our desired trademark is not already in use.
2. Trademark Application: Apply for a trademark with the appropriate government agency.
3. Intellectual Property Protection: Apply for patents, copyrights, or other forms of intellectual property protection as appropriate for our products.
4. Legal Review: Have a lawyer review all applications to ensure that they are legally sound and protect our interests.
5. Regular Monitoring: Regularly monitor the market to ensure that our trademarks and intellectual property rights are not being infringed upon.
